Upagraha'25

The department of ECE of Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering is proud to launch Its National level Technical Symposium, with the prime focus of encouraging young tech enthusiasts to engage in vigorous research and promote engineering to a whole new level. Upagraha'23 will act as a platform to exchange ideas and scientific knowledge through intense professional interaction. We believe this will lead to an inoculum of utilitarian ideas in various fields, not restricted to electronics!

We welcome you to participate and highlight your talent in front of eminent professors, industrial professionals, and talented students in a formal environment.
